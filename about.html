About Finclair

At Finclair, our mission is simple: empower business owners by giving them clarity and confidence in their finances.

Our Story

We’re Chris and Andrew—Tucson brothers who grew up helping our parents run local restaurants. After years of witnessing late-night number crunching and tax-season stress, we launched Finclair to give small-business owners the clean books and worry-free payroll we always wished our family had. You focus on serving customers and growing your brand; we’ll handle the bookkeeping, payroll, and taxes.

Our Mission

To empower small businesses with accurate data and expert guidance, turning finances from a stress point into a strategic asset.

Core Values

  • Clarity: Clear reports and straightforward pricing.
  • Integrity: We treat your business like our own.
  • Innovation: Leveraging tech to save you time and money.
  • Partnership: Your success is our success.
