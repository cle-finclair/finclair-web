About Us

At Finclair, our mission is simple: empower business owners by giving them clarity and confidence in their finances.

We’re Chris and Andrew — Tucson locals, brothers, and co-founders of Finclair. Growing up, we watched our parents run multiple restaurants here in Tucson. From working behind the counter to helping out during tax season, we’ve been immersed in the small business hustle our entire lives. We’ve seen the late nights, the margin juggling, and the stress that comes from trying to run a business while also managing the books.

We started Finclair because we know firsthand how critical clean, organized financials are — not just for peace of mind, but for growth. Whether you’re a restaurant owner, salon operator, or running any service-based business, your time is best spent doing what you do best: growing your brand and making money.

Let us take care of the bookkeeping, payroll, and taxes — so you can get back to business.
